# Sponsor Beta Plan - 2026-04-12

## Goal

Reach a repeatable sponsor beta motion that can produce KRW 500k to KRW 1,000k by month 6 without depending on broad SEO success.

This plan assumes the product wins with:

- a small number of high-intent local routes
- direct sponsor sales
- proof that the page already attracts rule-led operator demand

It does not assume site-wide SEO dominance.

## Revenue target math

The simplest path is not traffic scale. The simplest path is a small number of pilot sponsors.

Working target:

- 2 sponsors at KRW 250k to KRW 350k per month each
- or 3 sponsors at KRW 200k to KRW 300k per month each

That is enough to reach the month-6 target range without needing a large content footprint.

## What SEO must do

SEO still matters, but only in a narrow way.

The requirement is:

- a few route pages must rank well enough to show real impressions and some clicks
- the page must look trustworthy enough that a vendor believes the audience is real
- the page must convert operator intent into a call click, lead form, or sponsor inquiry

SEO does not need to carry the full business by itself for the current goal.

## Sales focus cities

Do not try to sell every city.

Primary sales cities:

- Charlotte
- Austin
- Miami

Do not sell Grand Island first.
Do not expand the city set before at least 2 sponsors are paying.

## Sales focus categories

Sell only three categories in the beta:

- grease haulers / grease trap service
- hood cleaners
- inspection-prep support only if a direct service offer exists

Inspection-prep should stay secondary until a service partner is clear.

## Weekly operating loop

### Every Monday

- review Search Console demand snapshots and route position changes
- review sponsored clicks, sponsor inquiries, and operator leads in `/admin`
- pick one primary route and one backup route for outreach that week

### Every Tuesday to Thursday

- send 5 new outreach emails per day
- call or form-submit 3 providers per day if direct email looks weak
- log every touch and reply in a simple spreadsheet or CRM

### Every Friday

- send follow-ups to all non-replies from 4 to 7 days ago
- review which pitch angle got replies
- update the next week's route priority

Minimum pace:

- 15 new outreach touches per week
- 10 follow-ups per week
- 1 live sponsor conversation per week by week 4

## Offer structure

Pilot offer only. Do not overcomplicate pricing.

### Pilot package A

- one city + one category
- sponsor slot on local rule and related service surfaces
- tracked call clicks
- sponsor inquiry routing
- monthly screenshot and click summary

Suggested pilot price:

- KRW 250k per month

### Pilot package B

- one city + two related categories
- sponsor slot priority
- tracked calls and leads
- monthly route summary

Suggested pilot price:

- KRW 350k to KRW 450k per month

## Outreach angle

Do not sell "SEO."
Sell "operators already looking for the exact compliance problem you solve."

Best pitch themes:

- rule-led local audience
- inspection-pressure traffic
- direct next-action routing
- no marketplace noise
